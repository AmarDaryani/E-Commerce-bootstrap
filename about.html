The way the web
was meant to be.

unique themes is the brainchild of our 3 founders: Avishai Abrahami, Nadav Abrahami and Giora Kaplan. The idea was born (on the beach) while they were building a website to be the basis of another start-up idea. The tech savvy trio quickly discovered that creating their own website was difficult, frustrating and very costly. It was this agonizing experience that led them to a light bulb moment—to build a platform that empowered anyone to create their own website with no coding or design skills needed.


In 2006, unique themes was born. Since then, we've evolved into the leader in website creation, committed to providing the latest features and capabilities that enable anyone to create a professional online presence.
